# Le Grand Saut — Declinaisons palette

> Meme scene (saut en costard, joie explosive, angle bas, editorial)
> Declinee en 10 palettes differentes pour tester dans Gemini.

---

## Scene de base

Personne en costard qui saute, bras ouverts, veste qui vole, joie pure, angle contre-plongeon, frozen mid-air.

---

## 1. Coral Energie (original)

```
Cinematic photorealistic image, artistic photo-montage, bold editorial style.
No text, no typography, no letters, no words anywhere.
Square format 1:1.

A person in a dark navy suit jumping in the air, captured at the peak of the jump, arms wide, jacket flying open. Explosive joy, a huge smile or a shout of freedom.

Vibrant coral red sky or backdrop (#E8453C). Strong side light creating sharp shadows. The person is frozen mid-air, defying gravity.

Dynamic angle — slightly from below, emphasizing the height. Fashion-editorial energy.
Mood: the exhilarating moment of the jump. Adrenaline, joy, no fear. A poster you'd hang on your wall.
```
