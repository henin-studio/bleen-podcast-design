# La Grande Démission — Prompts Gemini pour covers podcast

> 18 prompts (6 palettes x 3 scenes). Photo-montages graphiques, artistiques, direction artistique forte.
> Format cible : carré 1:1, sans texte (la typo est ajoutée en HTML).
> Chaque image sert de fond/visuel pour une cover podcast.

---

## Instructions communes (à coller avant chaque prompt)

```
Photorealistic artistic photo-montage for a podcast cover.
Cinematic lighting, strong art direction, editorial quality.
No text, no typography, no letters, no words, no logos anywhere in the image.
Square format 1:1, high resolution.
The image should work as a podcast cover background — visually striking even at small sizes (100px thumbnail).
Strong focal point, bold composition, graphic sensibility.
```

---

## Les 3 scenes

| Code | Scene | Brief Olivier |
|------|-------|---------------|

| **C** | **L'horizon** — personne de dos face a un paysage ouvert, mallette posée au sol, le moment d'après | Sobriete, futur desirable, contemplation active |

## Palette 1 — Nuit & Terre

**Ambiance :** bleu nuit profond, cuivre chaud, sable. Cinematique, introspectif, grave mais humain.

### P1-A — Papiers en l'air / Nuit & Terre

```
Cinematic photorealistic image, artistic photo-montage, editorial podcast cover quality.
No text, no typography, no letters, no words anywhere.
Square format 1:1.

A person in a dark business suit, seen from a slightly low angle, arms extended upward, releasing dozens of white paper sheets into the air. The papers catch the light as they scatter and float. The gesture is liberating, almost ceremonial — not angry, joyful.

Deep blue night atmosphere (#1B2838). Warm copper-toned side lighting illuminating the person's face and the floating papers. Papers glow warm sand (#E8D5B7) as they catch the light.

Cinematic depth of field — person sharp, background and some papers softly blurred.
Mood: the sacred moment of letting go. Serious but warm. A ritual of freedom.
```

```

Deep blue twilight sky (#1B2838) behind them. Warm copper (#C9956B) and golden light catching the edges of their suit and face — like golden hour just before night.

Motion blur on the feet, sharp focus on the upper body and expression.
```
